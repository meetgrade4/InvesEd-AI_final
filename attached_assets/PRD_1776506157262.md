## 1. Problem Statement

71% of Indian teens say they are interested in investing. Only 8% can correctly explain what a mutual fund is. Real brokerage apps require 18+, KYC, and real money. No safe sandbox exists where a 15-year-old can invest virtual money, experience a market crash, panic-sell, and learn why that was a mistake — without real financial risk.

**Competitors who fail to solve this fully:**
| Competitor | Biggest Gap |
|---|---|
| StockGro | No MF/SIP simulation, no behavioural AI, no certification |
| NSE Paathshala | College-focused, no AI, heavy textbook UI |
| Moneycontrol Virtual | Web-only, no gamification, no AI coaching |
| Moneypot | No AI, no MF, outdated, web-only |
| Zerodha Varsity | Learn-then-practice gap, no teen UX, no gamification |
| Investopedia | No Indian market data, USD-denominated |
| StockSim | India coming soon, no MF, no behavioural AI, not for teens |

## 7. InvesEd Simulator (M3)

### Portfolio Setup
- Starting capital: ₹1,00,000 (Free) / ₹10,00,000 (Pro)
- Earn additional capital via: course completions, quiz wins, daily logins
- Portfolio reset: allowed once every 30 days (marked in history)

### Investment Options

**Equities — NSE & BSE:**
- All Nifty 50, Nifty Next 50, and Sensex stocks
- Market orders, limit orders, stop-loss orders
- Real (Pro) / 15-minute delayed (Free) prices
- Sector filter: FMCG, IT, Banking, Pharma, Auto, Energy, Infra

**Mutual Funds:**
- All major AMCs: SBI MF, HDFC MF, ICICI, Mirae Asset, Axis, Parag Parikh, etc.
- Categories: large cap, mid cap, small cap, flexi cap, ELSS, debt, hybrid, index
- Lump sum + SIP setup (amount + frequency + duration)
- NAV updated daily, shows units, current value, XIRR

**SIP Simulator (Key Differentiator):**
- Select any fund → set monthly SIP → see historical NAV-based compounding
- "What if I had started in 2015?" — historical backtest for any fund
- Pause/increase/stop SIP → mirrors real SIP behaviour
- AI explains NAV fluctuations in plain language

**Market Crash Simulator:**
- Scenarios: 2008 Global Crisis, 2020 COVID, 2022 Rate Hike Selloff, Custom (Pro)
- Trigger → portfolio values tumble with animation → Hold / Sell / Buy More decision (15 sec timer)
- Outcome reveal: actual historical recovery timeline with comparison chart
- AI Coach message: behavioural coaching on the decision

### AI Behavioural Monitoring

Every trade and every research action is fed to the Behavioural Engine:

| Trigger | AI Response |
|---|---|
| Panic sell (selling during recoverable dip) | "You sold Reliance at -14%. In 8/10 similar corrections, it recovered within 60 days." |
| Chasing returns (buying stock up >30% in 30 days) | "You're buying at a 30% premium. Here's what happened after similar run-ups." |
| Over-concentration (one stock >40% of portfolio) | "47% in one stock. Diversification significantly reduces risk." |
| Research without action (3+ views, no invest) | "You've looked at HDFC Bank 3 times. What's holding you back?" |
| SIP lapse | "Your SIP has been paused 15 days. One more instalment = rupee cost averaging." |
| Great trade (held through drawdown, now positive) | "You held Wipro through a 12% dip and you're up 8%. That's long-term investing." |

## 8. Research Lab (M4)

### Stock Research — 6 Tabs

**Overview:** Price chart (1D/1W/1M/6M/1Y/5Y), volume bars, AI company summary, watchlist + invest buttons

**Fundamentals:** P/E, P/B, EPS, Revenue growth (3Y), Net profit growth (3Y), D/E, Dividend yield — all with "What does this mean?" hover tooltips and industry benchmark comparison

**Risk Profile (Key Differentiator):**
- Composite risk score 1–10
- 5-dimension breakdown: Volatility Risk | Business Risk | Financial Risk | Regulatory Risk | Market Sentiment
- "Risk vs Your Profile" card — compares stock risk to user's risk profile

**News & Sentiment:** Last 5 articles, AI sentiment score per article, 30-day sentiment trend chart

**Analyst View:** Buy/Hold/Sell consensus donut, target price range, AI bull/bear case summary

**FII/DII Activity:** Net buying/selling 30-day chart, promoter holding %, pledged % indicator, alert if >50% pledged

### Mutual Fund Research — 3 Tabs

**Performance:** Returns table (1Y/3Y/5Y/inception), NAV chart with SIP overlay, rolling returns chart, category rank

**Portfolio:** Top 10 holdings, sector allocation donut, top-10 concentration %

**Risk & Comparison:** Sharpe, Sortino, Max Drawdown, Beta, Alpha (with tooltips); compare up to 4 funds side-by-side; AI suitability tag + 100-word summary

### Research Intelligence Tracking (Silent)

All research logged: tabs viewed, time spent, watchlist additions, subsequent investment.
Used to generate **Research Intelligence Score (RIS)** and weekly AI research reports.

## 10. Situation Rounds (M6 — expanded)

Situation Rounds are curriculum-integrated checkpoints placed between Academy modules and Simulator features. The AI evaluates not just decisions but reasoning quality.

### Scoring
- Decision score (40%): Was the choice correct in hindsight?
- Reasoning score (40%): AI-evaluated open text reasoning (Claude)
- Speed score (20%): Faster decisions earn slight bonus
- XP: 200–500 per round depending on difficulty

### Bridge Round Map

| After Module | Bridge Round | Simulator Feature Unlocked |
|---|---|---|
| CF2 | COVID Crash — hold vs. sell | Full virtual portfolio |
| CF4 | SIP in a Bear Market | SIP setup + MF investing |
| CF5 | Auditor Resignation Alert | Research Lab Fundamentals |
| CF6 | Promoter Pledging Spike | Research Lab Risk Profile tab |
| AE1 | Insider Trading Rumour | Advanced charting (Pro) |
| AE2 | The Short-Seller Report | Full analyst + sentiment tab (Pro) |
| AE4 | Global Recession Contagion | Crash simulator custom scenarios (Pro) |

### Category 1 — Market & Economic Events
- **SR001** COVID Crash (March 2020) — Beginner — Your portfolio -35%. Hold/Sell/Buy?
- **SR002** RBI Rate Hike Shock — Intermediate — 50bps hike: how does it affect debt fund, IT stock, banking stock?
- **SR003** Budget Day Tax Shock — Intermediate — LTCG raised 10→15%. Sell now or hold?
- **SR004** SIP in a Bear Market — Beginner — Nifty -22% over 6 months. Friend says "stop SIP." What do you do?

### Category 2 — Geopolitical & Political Crisis
- **SR005** India-Pakistan Border Escalation — Sensex -3%. Defence stocks +8%. How do you react across your holdings?
- **SR006** Government Falls Mid-Term — Snap elections called. Nifty -5%. PSU bank -9%, FMCG -1%. Separate decisions for each.
- **SR007** Opposition Wins Surprise Majority — Infrastructure stocks -12%. IT flat. Your infra ETF is deep in red. Analyse policy risk vs. market overreaction.
- **SR008** US-China Trade War Escalation — US tariffs 145% on China. Indian IT uncertainty. Infosys -7%. Analyse bull case (supply chain shift) vs. bear case (project freezes).

### Category 3 — Corporate Corruption & Fraud
- **SR009** Auditor Resignation — Mid-cap you hold: auditor resigned "unable to verify transactions." Stock -11%. Management says routine. Sell? (AI cites Satyam, DHFL, IL&FS precedents)
- **SR010** Promoter Pledging Spike — Promoter pledging jumped from 30% to 72%. Stock hasn't reacted yet. You have 15% gain. What do you do?
- **SR011** The Short-Seller Report — 100-page report alleges accounting irregularities. Stock -25%. SEBI not acted. You're down ₹8,000. Sell, hold, or wait for regulatory clarity?
- **SR012** Insider Trading Rumour — Stock you research jumps 18% on no news. Options activity unusual day before. Acquisition announced next day. Buy the rally or flag the suspicious pattern?

### Category 4 — Macroeconomic Depression & Recession
- **SR013** India Enters Technical Recession — GDP negative 2 quarters. Unemployment 45-year high. Portfolio -28%. Sell, shift defensive, stay invested, or buy gold?
- **SR014** Global Financial Crisis Contagion — Major US bank collapsed. FIIs pulling ₹4,000 Cr/week. Sensex -40% YTD. Friend says "convert to gold." What do you actually do?
- **SR015** Stagflation — Inflation 9%, GDP 1.2%, rates high, equity flat, debt losing real value, gold +22%. Redesign your portfolio from scratch with ₹1,00,000. AI evaluates the mix.
- **SR016** Currency Crisis — Rupee 83→97 in 3 months. Import companies crashing, IT/pharma rallying. Your portfolio has no IT exposure. How do you rotate?

### Category 5 — Sectoral & Company Disruption
- **SR017** EV Disruption vs. Maruti — 2030 petrol ban announced. Maruti -18%, Tata Motors +11%. You hold Maruti with 22% gain. Hold or rotate to disruptor?
- **SR018** Pharma Patent Cliff — 60% of revenues from patent expiring in 18 months. Stock hasn't priced it. Sell ahead of cliff or hold on pipeline bet?
- **SR019** Bank NPA Crisis — NPA jumped 3%→7.5%. Management underreported in last call. Stock gaps -14% at open. Immediate response?
- **SR020** AIF Hedge Fund Short (Pro) — You are a Cat III AIF manager. Nifty at all-time high. Your mandate allows shorts. Which sector do you short and why? (AI evaluates thesis quality)

## 13. Advanced Investment Vehicles (M — PMS/SIF/AIF)

> **Core principle:** Teach these as knowledge, not products. A 16-year-old cannot invest in an AIF. Understanding how it works is essential financial literacy.

### PMS — Portfolio Management Services
- Real minimum: ₹50 lakhs (SEBI mandated)
- Types: Discretionary / Non-Discretionary / Advisory
- Fees: 1–2.5% annual + 10–20% profit share above hurdle rate
- **In simulator:** "Simulated PMS Account" managed by AI persona. After 30 days: "Your PMS charged 2% fee + 15% profit share. Net of fees: X%. Your own portfolio: Y%. Was the professional worth it?"

### SIF — Specialized Investment Funds
- Real minimum: ₹10 lakhs (SEBI 2024)
- Strategies not available to MFs: long-short equity, concentrated bets
- **In simulator:** "SIF Explorer" tab in Research Lab. Virtual allocation with live fee simulation.

### AIF — Alternative Investment Funds
- Real minimum: ₹1 crore
- **Category I:** Infrastructure, SME, social venture, angel funds
- **Category II:** Private equity, debt, fund of funds
- **Category III:** Hedge funds, leveraged, long-short, derivatives-heavy
- **In simulator:** AIF case studies (Zepto PE exit, distressed debt recovery, hedge fund short). SR020 Situation Round: play the role of a Category III AIF manager.

### Comparison Table (always shown)

| Feature | Mutual Fund | PMS | SIF | AIF |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Min. Investment | ₹500 SIP | ₹50 Lakhs | ₹10 Lakhs | ₹1 Crore |
| Investor Type | Any retail | HNI | Experienced retail | Institutional/UHNIs |
| Ownership | Units in pool | Direct stocks | Units in pool | Fund commitment |
| Liquidity | High | 30-day notice | Varies | Low (3–7yr lock-in) |
| Fee | 0.5–2% expense ratio | 1–2.5% + profit share | TBD | 2% + 20% carry |
| **Right for Teens Now?** | **Yes — start here** | No — learn about it | No — learn about it | No — learn about it |
